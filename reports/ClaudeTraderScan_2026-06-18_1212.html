What the signals mean (plain English)

Signal Plain English
MACD histogram positive & rising Recent momentum is shifting upward — buyers gaining strength. Early 'trend starting' hint.
RSI 50–65 Rising but not yet overbought. RSI 70+ = crowded, often due a pullback; below 30 = beaten down.
Stochastic %K above %D A faster momentum gauge agrees the move is up and isn't exhausted yet.
Price > SMA50 > SMA200 Price is above its 50-day average, which is above its 200-day: a confirmed uptrend, not a blip.
Volume expanding More shares trading than usual while price rises = real buying (accumulation).
3-month momentum +X% How much it already rose in 3 months. Confirms trend, but you're not buying the bottom.
Near 52-week high (breakout setup) Stocks pushing yearly highs often break through and keep going — but rejection is also common.
Historical hit rate Backtest on this stock: how often this same signal pattern led to +10% within 3 months. 75% still fails 1 in 4 times.
Revenue/earnings growth, margins, P/E, debt Business health: is the company growing, profitable, sanely priced, not over-borrowed?
