What the signals mean (plain English)

Signal Plain English

Volume expanding More shares trading than usual while price rises = real buying (accumulation).
3-month momentum +X% How much it already rose in 3 months. Confirms trend, but you're not buying the bottom.
Near 52-week high (breakout setup) Stocks pushing yearly highs often break through and keep going — but rejection is also common.
Historical hit rate Backtest on this stock: how often this same signal pattern led to +10% within 3 months. 75% still fails 1 in 4 times.
